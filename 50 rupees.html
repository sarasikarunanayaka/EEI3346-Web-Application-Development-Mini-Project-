Description.

The Central Bank of Sri Lanka printed Sri Lankan Rupee banknotes in 12 different denominations, including this 50 rupees Central Bank of Ceylon banknote (S.W.R.D. Bandaranaike). These belong to the series of discontinued Sri Lankan rupee banknotes. These 50 Sri Lankan Rupee banknotes were first printed in 1971 by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka. They were taken off the market in 1977.

S. W. R. D. Bandaranaike, Ceylon's fourth prime minister, is shown on the old 50 rupee currency. The Rs. 50 bill's reverse has a depiction of a tea plantation and tea pluckers.
