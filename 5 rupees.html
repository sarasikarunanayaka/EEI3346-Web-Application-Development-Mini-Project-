5 Rupees Banknote of the Central Bank of Ceylon.

Information.

  • Currency: Sri Lankan Rupees
  • Landmark: Polonnaruwa Vatadage, Stupa House
  • Material: paper
  • Colour: multicolour, red
  • Place: Polonnaruwa
  • Text: 5, Central Bank of Sri Lanka, Five Rupees
  • Object: flowers, round stupa building
  • Animal: butterfly

Description.

This 5 Sri Lankan Rupee banknote was one of 12 distinct denominations of Sri Lankan Rupee notes released by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka (Polonnaruwa Vatadage). These belong to the series of discontinued Sri Lankan rupee banknotes. These five Sri Lankan rupee banknotes were first printed by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka in 1982. They were taken off the market in 1991.

The round stupa structure from Sri Lanka's historic Polonnaruwa Vatadage complex is depicted on the orange-red five Sri Lankan rupee banknote. Five rupee coins have taken the place of the Rs. 5 bill.
